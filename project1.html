SMART BLIND STICK

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Without vision it can be challenging for a visually impaired person to navigate through a room or a hallway without bumping into obstacles. Even with aid,such as walking stick, it can be sometimes inconvenient, uncomfortable,and perhaps, inaccurate in avoiding obstacles.


DEFINATION OF THE PROJECT

Here,we develop a tool which can serve as a blind stick being more efficient and helpful than conventional one. The smart blind stick automatically detects the obstacle in front of the person and give him a response to the person by vibrating the stick and also with a warning sound. Through this, the blind person can aware about the obstacles in front of him. I used Ultrasonic sensor for detecting the obstacles.


IDEATE OF THE PROJECT

The main component is Arduino Uno .it contains everything needed to support the microcontroller Ultrasonic sensor is connected with Arduino broad,it senses the material or things which is near to the user. Here transmitters convert electrical signals into ultrasonic sound. Buzzer helps the user and even to the people who near to the user. We use these components to make a blind stick which is easy to carry and helpful for the blind people.


COMPONENTS

Arduino nano ( Any version will work ) Ultrasonic sensor HC - SR04 PVC pipe Buzzer and LED 9v batteries Battery connecter Cable tie clips  Jumper wires
